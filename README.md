🔍 Overview
HireAR AI is India's first full-stack GenAI-powered recruitment platform designed for small and mid-sized businesses and college placement cells.

Traditional hiring takes weeks, requires manual effort, and is costly. HireAR enables intelligent hiring in under 3 days, combining:
- AI-generated screening tests
- One-way video interviews
- Automated evaluation based on clarity, confidence, and relevance

💡 Problem We’re Solving
- Indian SMEs and colleges struggle with manual hiring
- Expensive platforms like TestGorilla or HireVue are unaffordable
- Candidates face long delays with no feedback

🚀 Solution – How HireAR AI Works

1. Smart MCQ Screening (Round 1)
- HR configures a role and gets a unique job code (e.g. 5139)
- Candidate enters code and uploads resume
- System generates **15 personalized MCQs** based on JD + resume
- 3-minute test auto-evaluated; only those who pass move to Round 2

2. One-Way Video Interview (Round 2)
- Candidates record answers to predefined questions
- AI scores them on:
  - Communication Clarity
  - Confidence
  - Relevance to Role
- AI recommends, HR decides

🧪 Real-World Use Case

- Saved over ₹43,000 and 40+ hours of HR time
